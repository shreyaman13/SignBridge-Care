# 1. Product Overview

SignBridge Care is an AI-powered healthcare communication platform designed to enable real-time conversations between healthcare professionals and patients who communicate using sign language or other accessible communication methods.

The platform focuses on removing communication barriers in hospitals by providing a fast, reliable, and privacy-focused communication experience.

Instead of replacing sign language interpreters, SignBridge Care assists healthcare professionals when an interpreter is not immediately available.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Millions of people with hearing and speech disabilities face communication barriers during medical consultations.

Current challenges include:

- Hospitals often do not have sign language interpreters available.
- Doctors and nurses generally do not understand sign language.
- Patients frequently depend on family members or guardians.
- Communication delays may affect patient care.
- Existing applications focus primarily on sign translation instead of complete conversations.

SignBridge Care aims to reduce these barriers by enabling direct communication between healthcare professionals and patients.

---

# 3. Vision

To ensure that no patient loses their independence because of a communication barrier.

---

# 4. Mission

To build intelligent communication technology that allows healthcare professionals and patients to communicate naturally, respectfully, and independently.

---

# 5. Target Users

Primary Users

- Deaf patients
- Speech-impaired patients
- Doctors
- Nurses
- Hospital reception staff

Secondary Users

- Hospitals
- Medical colleges
- Clinics
- Emergency healthcare workers

---

# 6. MVP Goal

Our first version should allow a doctor and a patient to complete a basic hospital consultation without requiring a sign language interpreter.

Success means a consultation can take place safely and efficiently.

---

# 7. Scope

Version 1 will focus only on communication inside hospital consultation rooms.

Features outside healthcare will be developed in future releases.

---

# 8. Success Metric

A doctor and patient should be able to complete a basic medical conversation within two minutes using SignBridge Care.
